Willow & Autism

Empower autistic children through tailored vocational training at Willow Child Development and Rehab Centre. Enhance employability, foster independence, and unlock their potential. Join us for a brighter future.

Empowering Autistic Children with Livelihood:

At Willow Child Development and Rehab Centre, we are proud to specialize in empowering autistic children by providing them with valuable skills for livelihood. We recognize the immense potential and unique strengths of autistic individuals and believe in creating opportunities for their personal growth and independence.

Our Vocational Training Program:

Through our specialized vocational training program, we focus on equipping autistic children with practical skills that enhance their employability and enable them to lead fulfilling lives. We offer tailored training in various vocational areas, taking into account their individual strengths, interests, and abilities. Our vocational training program encompasses a wide range of skills, including but not limited to:

  • Job-specific skills: We provide training in specific vocations based on the child's aptitude and interests. This may include areas such as computer programming, graphic design, culinary arts, woodworking, gardening, and more.
  • Social and communication skills: We emphasize the development of social and communication skills to enhance their ability to interact with colleagues, supervisors, and customers in a professional environment.
  • Independent living skills: We incorporate training in essential life skills, such as money management, time management, personal hygiene, and workplace etiquette, to foster independence and confidence.
  • Job readiness: Our program focuses on preparing autistic individuals for the job market, including resume building, interview skills, and job search strategies. We strive to empower them with the knowledge and tools necessary to secure and maintain employment successfully

Transforming Lives, Creating Opportunities:

By focusing on vocational training and empowering autistic children with livelihood skills, we aim to break down barriers and create a more inclusive society. We believe that every individual, regardless of their neurodiversity, deserves the opportunity to lead a productive and fulfilling life.

Our team of dedicated professionals combines their expertise in special education and vocational training to provide personalized support and guidance throughout the journey. We work closely with families, employers, and community partners to ensure a seamless transition from training to meaningful employment.

Join us at Willow Child Development and Rehab Centre as we make a difference in the lives of autistic children, transforming their potential into real-world opportunities. Together, let's create a society where every individual has the chance to thrive and contribute.
